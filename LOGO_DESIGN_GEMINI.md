# OtterSeal Logo Design - Gemini Generated Concept

**Generated:** 2026-02-02 via Gemini 3 Flash  
**Concept:** Otter + Security Seal Guardian

---

## 1. Visual Description

The logo features a **stylized, geometric otter** in a sitting position, hugging a large, circular **security seal** to its chest. The otter is rendered in flat, overlapping shapes with rounded corners to convey friendliness. Its eyes are simple dots, and a subtle "V" shape forms its mouth, giving it a content and reliable expression. 

The **Seal** is a hybrid design: a circular wax-seal base with a modern padlock shackle emerging from the top. Inside the seal's face is a **hexagonal "bit" pattern**, subtly nodding to block-based encryption and zero-knowledge proofs.

---

## 2. Design Elements & Proportions

*   **The Otter:** Occupies the primary vertical space (approx. 70% of total height). Built using two main circles for the head and body.
*   **The Seal (Lock):** Centered on the otter's torso. The diameter is 40% of the otter's total width.
*   **The Shackle:** A perfect semi-circle that loops just under the otter's chin, creating a "collar" effect that symbolizes protection.
*   **Typography:** A clean, geometric Sans-Serif (like Inter or Montserrat). "Otter" in semi-bold Indigo, "Seal" in bold Teal.

## 5. Scaling & Adaptability

*   **Full Logo:** Includes the otter, the seal, and the "OtterSeal" wordmark to the right. Used for headers and landing pages.
*   **Favicon (Small):** Only the circular **Seal** with the hexagon bit. This remains legible even at 16x16 pixels and represents the "locked" nature of the notes.
*   **Monochrome:** The otter and seal are reduced to high-contrast solid black/white shapes. The otter's features (eyes/mouth) and the hex-pattern are cut out as negative space to ensure the brand is recognizable in single-color printing or low-power displays.

---

## Design Rationale

- **Otter as Guardian:** The sitting, hugging pose conveys protection and care
- **Seal/Lock Symbolism:** Direct representation of security and encryption
- **Hexagon Pattern:** References cryptography and zero-knowledge proofs
- **Color Psychology:** 
  - Indigo = trust, security, tech
  - Teal = innovation, modernity, open-source
  - Amber = warmth, friendliness, approachability
- **Scalability:** Works at all sizes from 16px favicon to full website hero
- **Monochrome:** Maintains brand recognition in print and accessibility contexts
